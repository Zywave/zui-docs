Product branding

Proper logo use helps Zywave maintain a strong brand identity. Consistency is key and these guidelines will help you determine the best way to use our logos.

Standard Zywave corporate logos

The standard Zywave corporate logo should be used in most cases. The logo with a tagline is preferred over the one without. The Zycon is typically used as a subtle motif that reinforces our brand and should never be used in place of the Zywave logo.

Variations

Grayscale and white logo variations are generally preferred. White logos should appear on a field of color. Typically this will be one of our standard grays or Zywave green.

Clear space

Surround the Zywave logo with enough clear space so that it is clearly visible. This will maximize the recognition and impact of our identity. There are some instances—such as in our product headers—where including an ample amount of clear space is not necessary and can be omitted.

The Zywave chevron can be used to determine the appropriate amount of clearspace.

Logo abuse

It is extremely important to use our logo as described in these guidelines. Deviating from the prescribed usage compromizes the integrity of Zywave's brand. The examples shown here should be avoided at all costs.
